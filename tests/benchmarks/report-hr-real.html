    Market Insights

    1. Demand-Supply Mismatch: 250% YoY demand growth (2024–2025) vs. small active developer pool (2.6% Stack Overflow) suggests acute shortage.

    2. Remote Premium Significant: Remote work commands 2–3x local market rates; Argentina developers remote-hired at $1,200–$6,700/mo vs. local ≈$950/mo baseline.

    3. FinTech Concentration: RYZ Labs, Emma/Torre.ai, Improving South America actively recruiting senior roles—suggests consolidation in high-value verticals.

    4. Cost Arbitrage Viable: LATAM fully-loaded EOR cost $5,900–$7,150/mo (+ 10–29% employer tax) vs. global senior salary $165,000–$270,000 USD/year = significant margin.
