  • Junior: $1,000–$1,500/month ($12k–$18k/year)

  • Mid-level: $1,200–$3,000/month ($14.4k–$36k/year)

  • Senior: $2,500–$6,700/month ($30k–$80.4k/year)

    • Market Insights

    1. Talent concentration: Buenos Aires & São Paulo dominate listings; growing communities in Mexico & Colombia
    2. Skill requirements: 5+ years Scala standard for mid/senior roles; distributed systems + cloud (AWS/Azure/GCP) consistently required
    3. Niche premium: Scala developers earn 37% higher salary globally vs. general software engineers; LATAM remotely positions as cost-efficient specialist source
    4. Velocity: Salary data shows consistent pricing even across March 2026 snapshots; no sharp inflation

    Data Quality Assessment

    Strengths:

  • Multiple independent sources (LinkedIn, Glassdoor, Jooble, Get on Board, Indeed)

  • Consistent salary ranges across platforms where overlap exists

  • Recent postings confirm active 2026 hiring

    • Weaknesses:

  • Currency inconsistency: ARS/USD conversions not standardized; monthly vs. annual figures sometimes unclear

  • Date opacity: LinkedIn posting dates relative ("1 year ago") — exact dates unavailable for older listings

  • Duplicate overlap: Some companies/roles appear across multiple tables; unclear if unique or re-scraped

  • No volume: Total open positions not quantified; only qualitative "multiple roles" per source

  • Verification gap: No salary cross-validation between Glassdoor ARS and USD equivalents provided

    • Recommendation for future scrapes: Standardize to USD annual; capture absolute posting dates; deduplicate by job URL before aggregation.
