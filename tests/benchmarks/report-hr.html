# Scala Talent in LATAM: Research Brief

1) Key Findings

  • Hiring Growth: Scala job postings in LATAM grew 141.7% from Q1 2025 (120) to Q2 2026 (290), averaging +34 positions per quarter

  • Salary Bands:

    • - Junior: $16–28K
    - Mid: $40–65K
    - Senior: $60–100K
    - Lead: $85–170K
  • Talent Concentration: Argentina holds 45% of available positions; Brazil 25%; Colombia 15%

  • Seniority Mix: Job postings span Entry to Senior+ (8 posts analyzed), with Senior/Lead roles dominating sample

  • Location Flexibility: Mix of on-site Argentina and remote South America positions

    • 2) Market Insights

  • Strong sustained demand: quarterly growth remains consistent (14–25% per quarter) with no decline in trend

  • Significant salary overlap between Senior ($60–100K) and Lead ($85–170K) bands suggests role-based rather than tenure-based pricing

  • Argentina is regional hub; remote postings indicate willingness to tap broader LATAM talent

  • Skills required show convergence: Scala paired consistently with Java, Kotlin, DevOps, or backend specialization

    • 3) Data Quality Assessment

  • Limitations:

    • - Job posting sample (n=8) is small; geographic distribution (n=100) source unclear
    - Salary ranges from 3 sources (Howdy, Talently, Salancy, Glassdoor) lack date stamps and industry weighting
    - Chart salary figures differ slightly from range data (source of averages not documented)
  • Strengths:

    • - Trend data spans 6 consecutive quarters; linear fit shows consistency
    - Multiple job platforms included (LinkedIn, Jooble, Get on Board, etc.)
    - Clear geographic clustering supports Brazil as secondary talent hub
